"Dandelions" Sheet Music by Ruth B.

The pure sweetness of young love and longing is captured in the imagery of blowing on a dandelion to make a wish. But the song is more than that – it’s lyrical and dreamy; wistful and hopeful all at once.

The creation of Canadian singer and songwriter Ruth B, Dandelions was released in 2017, and it’s been growing in popularity ever since. It’s one of the most requested titles in our catalog. And it’s simply lovely.
