"Someone Like You" Sheet Music by Adele

Adele’s 2011 torch song “Someone Like You,” about a major heartbreak in Adele’s life, won the 2012 Grammy Award for Best Pop Solo Performance. The song was created by two people in two days in a studio. There were no synthesizers, no gimmicky production glitter. Unlike most torch songs, Adele is repairing her heart and working through the pain. The sheet music is available in original and easy versions
